The Synnovation Lab

Our mission is to deliver a meaningful learning experience to high school students through the use of project-based learning — where students take ownership of their learning, preparing them for the future.

What We Stand For

The Three Pillars of the Synnovation Lab

📖

Meaningful Learning

Students use the formative process in order to understand content and concepts at a deeper level. This allows students to prepare for summative assessments in a way that makes sense to them.

🎯

Personalization

The Synn Lab is personalized to each member through the use of one-on-one teaching, flexibility in how students present their knowledge, and the ability for students to suggest and make changes.

🤝

Community

The Synn Lab is one of the most tight-knit communities in Sycamore High School. The space and events are specially designed to promote collaboration and foster bonds between people across different grades.
